Letter from the Secretary General


Greetings delegates!

  On behalf of the RNPSMUN 2025 family, I am honoured to extend my warmest welcome to each and every one of you to the 9th edition of RNPSMUN. This MUN seeks to bring out the best in each delegate, in terms of both diplomacy and leadership, and I can assure you that by the end of these two days, not only would you have learnt a lot more than you expected, but also grown into a more confident and assertive person. The rigorous efforts which have gone in for the past numerous weeks reflect in what we all strive for – a glorious success of this conference. With four engaging committees, each with exciting agendas, a professional Executive Board, an enthusiastic Organizing Committee and a dedicated Secretariat, we promise you an educational and fun-filled conference, along with a great learning experience and lots of gratifying memories. From being a nervous first timer a few years ago to being the Secretary General of RNPSMUN 2025, the journey has been phenomenal, and I truly hope you find MUNing as delightful as I do. Regardless of whether you win or not, it is the impact you make that matters most. As future global citizens, it is our responsibility to find solutions for the various pressing issues that plague the world today, and change, after all, starts with small conversations among people who care. This conference is a small step that will go a long way. Please feel free to reach out to me in case of any queries. Looking forward to meeting you all!
What is RNPSMUN?


An amalgamation of consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented.

Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rational arbitrations will help sail you through! So delegates, come be a part of this exhilarating experience!
